Introduction

Make the best 5-card hand to win using exactly 2 of your 4 private cards and 3 of the 5 community cards.

Game Flow
  1. Each player receives 4 hole cards face down.
  2. Pre-flop betting round.
  3. Flop: 3 community cards revealed.
  4. Second betting round.
  5. Turn: 4th community card.
  6. Third betting round.
  7. River: 5th community card.
  8. Final betting round.
  9. Showdown: Players must use exactly 2 hole + 3 board cards.
Variants
  • Pot-Limit Omaha (PLO) ‚Äì Most common; max bet = pot size.
  • Omaha Hi-Lo (8 or Better) ‚Äì Pot split between best high and best qualifying low hand (5 unpaired cards, all ‚â§8).
  • 5-Card Omaha ‚Äì Players get 5 hole cards, must use exactly 2.
  • Big O ‚Äì 5-Card Omaha Hi-Lo.
  • No-Limit Omaha ‚Äì Rare; allows all-in at any time.

Strategy Guide
  • Rule of 2 & 3: Always use exactly 2 hole + 3 board cards.
  • Play for the Nuts: Second-best hands lose big pots in Omaha.
  • Hi-Lo Tip: Aim to scoop (win both high and low).
  • Position Matters: Play tighter from early position.
  • Stack Deep: PLO plays best with 100+ big blind stacks.
