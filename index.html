What is a Crypto Faucet?

A crypto faucet is a reward system that gives users small crypto amounts (known as satoshis for Bitcoin) in exchange for completing simple tasks such as captcha, short links, or ad views. Over time, faucets have evolved into multi-earning platforms offering passive income, staking rewards, and direct FaucetPay withdrawals.
